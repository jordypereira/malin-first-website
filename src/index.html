Cellist Malin Mertens

Norwegian Malin Mertens studied at Barratt Due Institute of Music in Oslo under Kari Ravnan and Ole Eirik Ree, followed by a master's degree with Louisa Tuck. While studying, she was active as a musician and has performed with several professional ensembles and orchestras, including the Oslo Philharmonic, the Norwegian Radio Orchestra and the Trondheim Symphony Orchestra.
