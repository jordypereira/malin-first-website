Cellist Malin Mertens

|

Malin Rosa Mertens studied her bachelor’s degree at Barratt Due Institute of Music in Oslo under Kari Ravnan and Ole Eirik Ree, followed by a master's degree with Louisa Tuck with final examination in 2019. She has worked with several professional ensembles and orchestras including the Oslo Philharmonic, Norwegian Opera & Ballet, the Norwegian Radio Orchestra, Royal Stockholm Philharmonic Orchestra and Arctic Philharmonic Orchestra. She was a founding member of the Virentia String Quartet performing in festivals and concerts both abroad and in Norway. During her master's degree she played on a cello lent by Dextra Musica foundation. After her master's degree she worked one year with the Royal Stockholm Philharmonic Orchestra as an orchestra academist (RSPO Orchestra Academy). Her passion for playing chamber music has resulted in many projects and concerts, being the instigator for creating experiences and sharing classical music. Since fall 2020 she has been working as a full-time freelance cellist based in Oslo, Norway.

Malin Mertens studerte ved Barratt Due musikkinstitutt i Oslo under Kari Ravnan og Ole Eirik Ree, etterfulgt av en mastergrad med Louisa Tuck med avsluttende eksamen i 2019. Under studietiden var hun aktiv som musiker og har opptrådt med flere profesjonelle ensembler og orkestre, inkludert Oslo Filharmonien, Arktisk Filharmoni, Kungliga Filharmonikerna i Stockholm, Kringkastingsorkesteret og Trondheim Symfoniorkester. Hun var en av grunnleggerne av Virentia Strykekvartett, og i løpet av mastergraden spilte hun på en cello utlånt av Dextra Musica. Etter mastergraden jobbet hun ett år med Kungliga Filharmonikerna i Stockholm som orkesterakademist. Siden høsten 2020 jobber hun som frilanser på heltid med base i Oslo, Norge.
